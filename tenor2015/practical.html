TENOR 2015
First International Conference on Technologies for Music Notation and Representation

28-30 May 2015 - Paris - France


Practical Information


Accommodation information is below

Locations

The conference is hosted by Paris-Sorbonne University and Ircam.

Papers sessions and keynote will take place at:

Maison de la Recherche - Room D035
28 rue Serpente 75006 Paris
Métro : Odeon (Line 4 or 10)


Posters and Papers/Music session (Friday afternoon) will take place at:

Ircam - Salle Stravinsky
1 Place Igor-Stravinsky 75004 Paris
Métro : Hotel de Ville (Line 1 or 11) or Chatelet (Line 1, 4, 7, 11, 14)

Going from Maison de la Recherche to Ircam takes less than 20 minutes walking and there are easy subway connections too.


Accommodation

Hotels in Paris are generally rather small and it's safer to book in advance. The 6th arrondissement of Paris (where the main of the conference takes place) is an expensive location. That's why none of the proposals below are close to the Maison de la Recherche. However, most of them are at walking distance or at 10 to 15 minutes using public transportations.

Here are some nice and charming places close to Ircam, around Beaubourg and “Le Marais“:
  • Hôtel du Cygne
    20 rooms in a 17th century building - 130-165€
  • Hôtel Ecole Centrale
    22 rooms near Arts-et-Métiers - 108-189€
  • Hôtel Beaubourg
    28 rooms - 110-220€
Farther and cheaper, a nice hotel:
  • Hôtel Paris France
    48 rooms - 75-146€ (with triple beds)
Close to Gare du Nord station (on metro line 4 and RER line from CDG airport):
  • Hôtel Libertel Gare du Nord Suède
    50 rooms - 80-130€
A new freshly re-designed youth hotel:
  • Generator Paris (10th arrondissement)
    920 beds! Shared and individuals room - 25-128€
For other places and if you search by yourself, you should find reasonable solutions close to the "Quartier du Marais" or "Hotel de ville" or "Louvre".
